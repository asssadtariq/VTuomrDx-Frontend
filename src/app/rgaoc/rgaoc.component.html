Research Gap

Numerous methods have been suggested and examined in the literature for the detection and classification of brain tumors to expand the possibilities of treatment and the endurance of the patients. The majority of the studies have focused more on binary-model classification, with less attention paid to multi-model classification. In literature for classification, we have not seen any article in which they have shown results on the datasets that haven't used in training. We have created a platform that has enhanced the decision-making and precision of medical professionals when diagnosing brain tumors managing brain tumor segmentation and multi-class estimation.

Our Contribution

We have carried out our research in light of multiple research studies based on multi-class models and have achieved good accuracy for the datasets which we haven't used in training. This project aimed to develop a model to classify types of brain tumors using deep learning techniques. We have explored & used various:-

  • Image processing
  • Image segmentation
  • Feature extraction
  • Deep Learning techniques

to come up with a more usable dataset that produces higher accuracy.
